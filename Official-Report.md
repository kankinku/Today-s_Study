- Greenland location = optimal for Russian ICBM coverage
- Pacific region = optimal for Chinese missile coverage
- Coverage = continuous global presence

**Current Status:**

**Concept:** Destroy warheads after re-entry (last resort)

**Systems:**
- THAAD (Tactical High Altitude Area Defense)
- PAC-3 (Patriot Advanced Capability-3)
