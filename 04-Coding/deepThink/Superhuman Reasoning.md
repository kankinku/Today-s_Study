### [IMO Bench](https://github.com/google-deepmind/superhuman/blob/main/imobench/README.md)

[](https://github.com/google-deepmind/superhuman#imo-bench)

A suite of advanced benchmarks designed to evaluate robust mathematical reasoning in AI. Following our [2025 IMO-gold achievement](https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/advanced-version-of-gemini-with-deep-think-officially-achieves-gold-medal-standard-at-the-international-mathematical-olympiad/), this release includes:

- _IMO-AnswerBench_: 400 challenging short-answer problems.
    
- _IMO-ProofBench_: 60 proof-based problems vetted by experts.
    
- _IMO-GradingBench_: A dataset of 1000 human gradings to advance automatic evaluation.
    

## [Aletheia](https://github.com/google-deepmind/superhuman/blob/main/aletheia/README.md)

[](https://github.com/google-deepmind/superhuman#aletheia)

A math research agent, powered by Gemini Deep Think, that can iteratively generate, verify, and revise solutions. See [paper](https://github.com/google-deepmind/superhuman/blob/main/aletheia/Aletheia.pdf).

This release includes prompts and outputs from Aletheia on research level math problems.
